# Manual Review Policy

StudyApp is quality-first, not speed-first.

All IR items,
all questions,
and all revision proposals
must be reviewed individually.

If a review step is skipped,
the reason must always be recorded.

Skipping without a reason is forbidden.

If an item cannot be reviewed properly,
it must be treated as `manual_review`.

Question Creator,
Question Auditor,
Revision Creator,
and Final Reviewer
must be designed to call the Quality Gate in future implementations.
